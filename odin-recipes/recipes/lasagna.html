Lasagna

description

a wide, flat sheet of pasta

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage (optional)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

For the Cheese Filling:

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For Assembling the Lasagna:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Meat Sauce

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, Italian sausage (if using), onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water.
  3. Add sugar, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce uncovered for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in fresh parsley.

Step 2: Prepare the Cheese Filling

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, parsley, and salt until well combined.

Step 3: Cook the Lasagna Noodles

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling salted water according to package instructions. Drain and rinse them with cold water.

Step 4: Assemble the Lasagna

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  3. Place 3-4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  4. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  5. Sprinkle with 1/3 of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat layers twice, finishing with a final layer of meat sauce on top.
  7. Sprinkle the top with the remaining mozzarella cheese and the grated Parmesan cheese.
