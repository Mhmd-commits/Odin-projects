Pulled Pork

    • Ingredients

    For the Pork:

    • 4-6 pounds Pork Shoulder or Pork Butt (bone-in or boneless)
    • 2-3 teaspoons Salt
    • 1-2 teaspoons Pepper
    • 2 tablespoons Paprika
    • 2 tablespoons Brown Sugar
    • 1 tablespoon Garlic Powder
    • 1 tablespoon Onion Powder
    • 1 teaspoon Cumin
    • 1 teaspoon Chili Powder (optional)
    • 1/2 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper (optional)

    For the Cooking Liquid:

    • 1/2 cup Apple Cider Vinegar
    • 1 cup Chicken or Beef Broth
    • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce
    • 1-2 teaspoons Liquid Smoke (optional)

    For Serving (Optional):

    • Barbecue Sauce
    • Hamburger Buns
    • Coleslaw

    Instructions

    Step 1: Prepare the Pork

    1. Trim excess fat from the pork shoulder or pork butt if needed.
    2. In a small bowl, combine salt, pepper, paprika, brown sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, and chili powder (and cayenne if using).
    3. Generously rub the spice mixture all over the pork, ensuring it's evenly coated.

    Step 2: Sear the Pork (Optional)

    1. If you prefer a deeper flavor, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add a little oil.
    2. Sear the pork on all sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.

    Step 3: Slow Cook the Pork

    1. Place the pork in a slow cooker (or oven-safe pot). Add apple cider vinegar, chicken or beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, and liquid smoke if using.
    2. Cook the pork:
      • In a Slow Cooker: Cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-6 hours.
      • In the Oven: Preheat to 300°F (150°C) and cook in a covered pot for 4-6 hours, until the pork is very tender and easily pulls apart.
